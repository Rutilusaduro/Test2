# Feast of the Glutton God — Game Overview

A reference summary of the setting, core loop, and mechanical systems as implemented in the codebase. For authoring prose, see `AUTHORING.md`. For tuning knobs, see `TUNING.md`.

---

## Setting & tone

**Gorgara the Everfull** — an ancient goddess of abundance — is awakening across **the Everfull Continent**. Her power manifests as irresistible, pleasurable growth: bodies swell with beauty and strength, regions transform, and devotion spreads through feasts, magic, and intimacy.

You play the **Chosen of Gorgara**: a divine spark-bearer who wanders a semi-open world, growing companions and NPCs, reshaping regions, and fighting (or converting) those who resist abundance — especially the **Purity** hardliners who hunt “indulgent” magic.

**Tone:** Sensual, positive, empowering. Growth is treated as power and pleasure, not shame. Recent systems add **repercussions** for forcing growth on unwilling NPCs — the world pushes back, but redemption is always possible.

**Tech:** React + Vite browser RPG. 100% offline; saves to `localStorage`. Nearly all player-facing text is generated by a **modular text engine** (pools + selectors), not hardcoded strings.

---

## Core gameplay loop

1. **Travel** between connected regions on the continent map.
2. **Meet people** — strangers appear as sensory descriptors until you talk to them; then names, size, and bonds unlock.
3. **Interact** — talk, flirt, observe, feed, bless, feast, class Special actions, intimate scenes, corrupt/recruit.
4. **Grow** NPCs (and yourself) through feeding, blessings, spells, quests, and combat feeding.
5. **Spend resources** — Abundance Points (AP), spell slots, and **Gorgara's Favor** (daily growth-magic ceiling).
6. **Fight** on a 10×10 tactical grid when you seek encounters or when hostility/travel danger triggers combat.
7. **Advance** quests, regional transformation, abundance spread, and world reactivity (landmarks, blocked routes, faction shifts).
8. **Rest** — long rest restores HP, spell slots, partial AP, Favor, and satiation decay; advances the day.

A scripted **DM narrator** (not an LLM) frames arrivals, reacts to events, and offers soft hints when you seem stuck.

## Player character

### Classes (4)

| Class | Subclass | Fantasy |
|-------|----------|---------|
| Bard | Feast-Singer | Songs that swell allies and charm enemies |
| Wizard | School of Overflow | Arcane caloric transmutation |
| Cleric | Domain of Plenty | Divine blessings and living altars |
| Warlock | Pact of the Everfull | Hunger pacts and essence drain |

Each class has distinct spell lists, hit dice, saves, and a **Special** overworld action (signature growth magic; once per NPC per day; costs Favor).

### Progression

- **Level 1–12** — XP from combat, feeding, quests, growth milestones.
- **Size cap** — player growth is gated by level (`sizeCap`), unlike NPCs:
  - Lv 1 → stage 3 · Lv 3 → 5 · Lv 5 → 7 · Lv 7 → 9 · Lv 9 → 11 · Lv 12 → 13
- **Spell slots** — per-level slots; cantrips free; can overflow with AP.
- **ASI / features** — level-up choices at standard breakpoints.

### Resources

| Resource | Role |
|----------|------|
| **HP** | Combat survival; scales with size stage |
| **AP (Abundance Points)** | Blessings, feasts, some spell overflow, travel in command mode |
| **Spell slots** | Leveled spell casting (combat + overworld) |
| **Favor** | Daily ceiling for overworld growth magic; refilled by **Eat/Indulge** and long rest; `favorMax` grows as you fatten others (+1 max per 2 total stages granted worldwide) |

## Size stages — the central mechanic

Weight is tracked as **lbs** and mapped to **15 discrete stages** (0–14), from **Human** to **Tarrasque Matriarch**. Stages are the primary axis for prose, combat footprint, mobility, and world reactions.

### Stage ladder

| ID | Label | Band | ~Min lbs | Tier theme |
|----|-------|------|----------|------------|
| 0 | Human | Mortal | 80 | Slender starting point |
| 1 | Curvy | Mortal | 110 | Soft curves emerging |
| 2 | Plump | Mortal | 140 | Real belly, thighs pressing |
| 3 | Voluptuous | Mortal | 175 | Prominent belly, unmistakable presence |
| 4 | Massive | Heroic | 220 | Doorways feel narrow |
| 5 | Ogre | Heroic | 280 | Ogre-scale softness + strength |
| 6 | Giant | Heroic | 350 | Tower over mortals |
| 7 | Dragon | Mythic | 450 | Dragon-scale immensity |
| 8 | Leviathan | Mythic | 600 | Halls shrink around you |
| 9 | Behemoth | Mythic | 800 | Movement labored; influence effortless |
| 10 | Great Whale | Colossal | 1050 | World-scale reactions begin |
| 11 | Monolith | Colossal | 1400 | **Immobile** — command play |
| 12 | Titan | World | 1900 | Living landmark; cults form |
| 13 | World Mother | World | 2600 | Continental presence |
| 14 | Tarrasque Matriarch | World | 3500 | Apotheosis of gluttony |

**Bands:** Mortal → Heroic → Mythic → Colossal → World. Higher bands shift play from personal mobility toward **command, influence, and ritual**.

### How weight interacts with systems

#### Combat (grid)

| Stage range | Grid tiles | Movement/turn | HP bonus |
|-------------|------------|---------------|----------|
| 0–4 | 1×1 | 4 → 3 | — |
| 5–8 | 2×2 | 2 | +30% → +80% |
| 9–11 | 3×3 | 1 → 0 | +80% → +150% |
| 12+ | 4×4 | 0 | up to +400% |

- Larger units **occupy more cells** — tactical blocking and reach matter.
- **Growth mid-combat** restores HP and can shift tile control.
- Victory: kill, **convert** via overfeeding, or overwhelm with abundance.
- **Combat growth on enemies is exempt from consent** — fights don't ask permission.

#### Overworld mobility

| Stage | Movement | Structure access |
|-------|----------|------------------|
| 0–5 | Normal (4) | Normal doors/paths |
| 6–8 | Reduced (2–3) | Wide structures |
| 9–10 | Very low (1) | Vast spaces |
| 11+ | **0 (immobile)** | Ritual/command only |

At **stage 11+** you enter **Command Mode**: personal travel stops; companions delegate travel, or ritual projection unlocks later. You rule through decree, feasts, and devoted followers.

#### Stage perks (selected unlocks)

Perks stack by minimum stage — examples:

- **Stage 4+ (Plush Presence):** self-feed in combat, shoulder through obstacles, social bonuses.
- **Stage 6+ (Giant Dominion):** extra reach/damage/AC, living-bridge puzzle solutions, growth decree influence.
- **Stage 8+ (Mythic Aura):** body slam, growth damage bonuses, tight-space penalties begin.
- **Stage 10+:** colossal world-reaction tier; regional footprint accrues faster.
- **Stage 11+:** becomes a **living landmark** (snapshot stored even when NPC isn't loaded).
- **Stage 12+:** landmark tier 2, cult formation, faction/purity shifts in some regions.

#### World reactivity (persistent)

When growth crosses **stage 10+** in a region, the world **remembers**:

- **Region footprint** — accrues `tileSize²` per growth; crowded prose at ≥6; can **block a travel exit** at ≥12; **impassable to small travelers** at ≥20.
- **Living landmarks** — characters at stage ≥11 become fixed POIs with name, locale, cult flags, settle state.
- **Settling** — over days without new growth, regions calm; blocked routes can reopen (settle state 0→1→2).
- **Faction flags** — e.g. Purity weakened in Gilded Citadel / Ember Duchy when titans rise.
- **Rumor queue** — notable acts surface in NPC dialogue.
- **Living ledger** — named milestones for endgame / NG+ flavor.

Growth is applied through a central pipeline (`applyGrowthWithPresentation` → optional `notifyWorldReaction`). NPC overworld growth also runs **consent + satiation** checks first.

## NPC model — four axes

Each NPC/companion tracks several **independent** stats that drive both mechanics and prose:

### 1. Relationship (0–100) — bond with *you*

| Tier | Min | Label |
|------|-----|-------|
| 0 | 0 | Neutral |
| 1 | 15 | Friendly |
| 2 | 35 | Close |
| 3 | 55 | Intimate |
| 4 | 75 | Craving |
| 5 | 90 | Devoted |

Unlocks interactions (flirt at 1, bless/special at 2, feast/intimate at 3). Higher tiers add **growth stage bonuses** on feed/bless/intimate. Devoted grants satiation tolerance.

### 2. Gain-Desire (0–100) — willingness to grow

| Tier | Min | Label |
|------|-----|-------|
| 0 | 0 | Reluctant |
| 1 | 25 | Open |
| 2 | 50 | Eager |
| 3 | 75 | Insatiable |

Seeded by archetype/body type. Raised by consensual growth, flirt, devotion; lowered by forced growth and satiation violations. Drives the text-engine **`attitude`** dimension (feelings about gaining).

### 3. Corruption (0–100) — acceptance of abundance

| Tier | Min | Label |
|------|-----|-------|
| 0 | 0 | Resistant |
| 1 | 34 | Curious |
| 2 | 67 | Enthusiastic Gainer |

Legacy axis; still used by some prose selectors. Distinct from Gain-Desire.

### 4. Satiation (0–100) — fullness / overwhelm

| Tier | Min | Effect |
|------|-----|--------|
| 0 | 0 | Comfortable — full growth |
| 1 | 25 | Full — diminishing returns |
| 2 | 50 | Stuffed — locks feed/bless/special/intimate |
| 3 | 75 | Overwhelmed — temporary **refusal** |

Rises with each growth event (more for bigger jumps); decays on long rest and across days. **Applies regardless of willingness** — even eager NPCs push back if rushed. Devoted NPCs tolerate more before refusal (threshold 90 vs 75).

### Consent (graduated)

Overworld/companion growth checks **consent budget** = `min(gainDesire, relationship)`:

| Budget | Comfortable stages per action |
|--------|-------------------------------|
| 0–14 | 0 |
| 15–39 | 1 |
| 40–59 | 2 |
| 60–79 | 3 |
| 80+ | 4 |

Exceeding comfort = **forced growth**:

- Fewer stages actually applied (resistance sheds magic)
- Relationship + Gain-Desire loss
- Forced prose instead of celebratory pools
- **Region hostility** signal logged (see below)

## Repercussions — world & scarcity (3A + 3B)

### Region hostility (hidden per region, 0–100)

Fed by forced-growth events. Four public tiers:

| Tier | Min | Effects |
|------|-----|---------|
| Calm | 0 | — |
| Wary | 25 | 50% relationship gains; some archetypes decline talk |
| Tense | 50 | Hostile encounters on travel (Inquisitors, rivals) |
| Crackdown | 75 | Feed/bless/feast/special **locked**; redemption quest unlocks |

Decays slowly over days. Completing **Amends to the Hearth** (communal feast + defeat inquisitor + mend bonds) clears crackdown and drops hostility.

### Gorgara's Favor

- Overworld growth, blessings, and Special cost Favor (1–4 depending on magnitude).
- Empty Favor = growth magic **unavailable** (hard daily ceiling).
- **Eat / Indulge** action restores Favor (once per day until rest).
- Special: **once per NPC per day**.

## Combat

- **10×10 grid**, turn-based action economy (Action / Bonus / Reaction / Movement).
- **DM combat scenes** — blocking intro before fight, wrap-up after victory/defeat/conversion.
- **Spells** — all cost Action or Bonus; slots or AP; targeting modes (single, AoE, self).
- **Feed** — in-combat growth on targets; conversion track on enemies.
- **Enemy inspector** — tap to select, confirm attack; HP bars on cells.
- **Mobile layout** — sticky action bar, responsive grid.

## Text engine (why descriptions feel alive)

All major scenes route through `render(template, createContext({ subject, ref, globals }))`.

- **Pools** — registered variant lists with `when:{ selector }` filters.
- **Dimensions** — `stage`, `relationship`, `gainDesire`, `satiationTier`, `attitude`, `bodyType`, `archetype`, `corruption`, `hostilityTier`, `favorState`, etc.
- **Scenes** — `npc.feed`, `npc.talk`, `dm.arrival`, `dm.combat.*`, `player.indulge`, growth events, quests, landmarks, and more.
- **Pronouns** — engine uses `{they}/{their}/{verb:lemma}` agreement, not hardcoded she/her.

`npm run text:lint` validates pool length, selectors, and runs render sweeps.
